Each LO signals a guiding waypoint. They clarify what students should be able to do by the end of the course sequence.

  • LO2 Apply core science and engineering principles to analyze real-world problems.
  • LO3 Design efficient algorithms under resource constraints.
  • LO4 Implement and evaluate software solutions using appropriate tools and methods.
  • LO5 Integrate hardware and software components for embedded or connected systems.
  • LO6 Address security, ethics, and societal impact in engineering decisions.
  • LO7 Communicate technical ideas effectively in written and oral form.
  • LO8 Lead collaborative software or project teams using modern tooling.
  • LO9 Engage in self-directed learning and professional development.
